<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: center"><B>JOINT FILING AGREEMENT</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: center">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-indent: 0.5in">This JOINT FILING AGREEMENT (this &ldquo; Agreement&rdquo;),
dated as of January 1, 2025, is entered into by and among Bristol Investment Fund, Ltd., a Cayman Islands exempted company (&quot;Bristol
Fund&quot;) and Bristol Capital, LLC, a Delaware limited liability company (&quot;BC&quot;) (Bristol Fund and BC are collectively referred
to herein as the &ldquo;Joint Filers&rdquo;).</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-indent: 0.5in">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-indent: 0.5in">WHEREAS, each of the Joint Filers have filed or is
filing a Schedule 13G under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 (the &ldquo;Exchange Act&rdquo;) with respect to securities of Nuvve Holding
Corp. (the &ldquo;Schedule 13D&rdquo;);</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-indent: 0.5in">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-indent: 0.5in">WHEREAS, each of the Joint Filers is individually eligible
to file a Schedule 13D;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-indent: 0.5in">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-indent: 0.5in">WHEREAS, each of the Joint Filers wishes to file an
amendment to the Schedule 13D jointly and on behalf of each of the Joint Filers, pursuant to Rule 13d-1(k)(1) under the Exchange Act;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-indent: 0.5in">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-indent: 0.5in">NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of these premises
and other good and valuable consideration, the parties hereto agree as follows:</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-indent: 0.5in">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-indent: 0.5in">1. The Joint Filers hereby agree that the Schedule
13G is, and any amendments thereto will be, filed on behalf of each of the Joint Filers pursuant to Rule 13d-1(k)(1) under the Exchange
Act.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-indent: 0.5in">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-indent: 0.5in">2. Each of the Joint Filers hereby acknowledges that,
pursuant to Rule 13d-1(k)(1)(ii) under the Exchange Act, it is responsible for the timely filing of the Schedule 13G and any amendments
thereto, and for the completeness and accuracy of the information concerning it contained therein, and is not responsible for the completeness
and accuracy of the information concerning any of the other parties contained therein, unless it knows or has reason to know that such
information is inaccurate.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-indent: 0.5in">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-indent: 0.5in">3. Each of the Joint Filers hereby agrees that this
Agreement shall be filed as an Exhibit to the Schedule 13G, pursuant to Rule 13d-1(k)(1)(iii) under the Exchange Act.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-indent: 0.5in">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-indent: 0.5in">4. This Agreement
may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original and all of which, taken together, shall constitute but one
and the same instrument, which may be sufficiently evidenced by one counterpart.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-indent: 0.5in">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-indent: 0.5in">IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties hereto have caused
this Agreement to be executed as of the day and year first above written.</P>
